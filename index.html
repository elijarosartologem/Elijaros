About Me

Welcome to my creative world of phoneography and artistic filters! Hello, I'm Elijaros, a passionate artist who finds beauty in the everyday moments captured through the lens of my trusty telephone. Armed with nothing more than my smartphone and an eye for aesthetics, I've discovered the enchanting realm of phoneography, transforming ordinary scenes into extraordinary works of art. With each snap of the shutter, I embark on a journey of visual storytelling, bringing life to my surroundings through a unique perspective. My love for photography has taken me to unexpected places, unveiling the hidden wonders that often go unnoticed. The real magic, however, lies in the art of post-processing. Armed with an array of carefully curated filters and editing techniques, I add a touch of my creativity, enhancing the mood, colors, and textures, to reveal the essence of my vision. Through this online portfolio, I invite you to explore my ever-growing collection of photographs and witness the world as I see it – an extraordinary tapestry of emotions, colors, and narratives. Whether it's the soft hues of a sunset, the vibrant chaos of a bustling street, or the tranquil serenity of nature, each image is a testament to the beauty that surrounds us. I hope my work sparks joy, ignites curiosity, and perhaps even inspires you to embrace your creativity. Feel free to browse through my portfolio and get in touch if you'd like to collaborate, share ideas, or simply say hello! Thank you for joining me on this artistic journey, and remember, beauty is all around us – sometimes, we just need to capture it through the lens of our hearts. Warmest disregards, Elijaros
